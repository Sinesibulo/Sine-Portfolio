[ About Me ]

Hey there! I'm Sinesipho Sibulo, a Data & Business Analyst with a National Diploma in Information and Communications Technology (Business Analysis) from Walter Sisulu University (2024). I'm currently a Transformation Data Analyst Intern at Naspers/Prosus, working across the Transformation and Naspers Labs space — from Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E) and B-BBEE transformation analytics to spend analysis, financial reconciliations, and building web tools and dashboards used across the business.

Before Naspers, I completed a year-long AI & Data Engineering internship at CAPACITI (UVU Africa), where I built data pipelines, cleaned and preprocessed datasets for machine learning models, developed NLP and analytics dashboards, audited ML models for bias, and shipped AI-powered web apps — earning two Candidate Spotlight nominations along the way. At Naspers I've run monthly B-BBEE compliance reporting cycles, supported a full financial-year audit close-off across multiple auditor streams, and coordinated the Naspers Labs Youth Mentorship programme end-to-end, including designing and building its mentor-booking website. I use AI tools like Claude, Gemini, and Toqan daily to automate reporting, analysis, and document production.

I work across Python, Power BI, SQL, Excel, and modern web technologies, and I enjoy translating technical concepts into clear language for stakeholders. I'm looking to grow in data analysis, business analysis, and IT/tech roles where AI and data-driven thinking create real impact.
